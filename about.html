History of THEGYM

Renovated to be Charlotte's best location for fitness, THEGYM has an expansive 5 acres of land. Formerly a shopping mall with 3 department stores, THEGYM has tons of space for all of your fitness goals to come to life. What was once a department store has transformed into a Ninja Warrior gym complete with a rock climbing wall. Walking aisles from the shopping mall have now been transformed into an indoor track, allowing you to run in all seasons and weather. Join now to get a membership with THEGYM: the gym of the future.
